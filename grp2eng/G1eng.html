STEAMtopia MED Guidelines

About Our Program

STEAMtopia MED is an innovative educational platform designed to introduce children aged 6-8 to medical and scientific concepts in a fun and engaging way. Our program combines theoretical learning with hands-on activities to stimulate children's natural curiosity.

Important Note: Our content is designed for education and entertainment purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice. For any medical concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Safety & Privacy

Data Protection

We take your personal data protection very seriously:

  • We only collect strictly necessary information
  • All data is stored securely
  • We never share your information with third parties without consent

Online Safety

To ensure a safe experience:

  • All online sessions are moderated
  • Chats and interactions are monitored
  • We use secure platforms for our virtual sessions

Session Participation

Preparing for Sessions

To get the most out of our sessions:

  1. Check your internet connection before the session
  2. Prepare necessary materials (list provided in advance)
  3. Create a quiet, comfortable space for the child
  4. Connect 5 minutes before session start time

Code of Conduct

We expect all participants to:

  • Show respect to teachers and other participants
  • Engage in active, constructive participation
  • Use communication tools appropriately
  • Follow safety instructions during experiments

Technical Support

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Audio issues: Check your audio settings and headphone connections
  • Video problems: Ensure your camera is enabled in settings
  • Unstable connection: Try moving closer to your router or reducing video quality
  • Accessing materials: Check your download folder or contact us

Contact Us

For any questions or technical issues:

  • Email: steamtopia.team@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 24 business hours
