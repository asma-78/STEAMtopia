Frequently Asked Questions

Is STEAMtopia MED free?

Yes, our platform is completely free! We believe that quality science education should be accessible to all children. However, you can support us financially if you wish, or simply by sharing our social media with friends and family.

How can I support STEAMtopia MED?

You can support us in several ways:

  • By sharing our content on social media
  • By telling others about us (parents and teachers)
  • By making a donation through our support page
  • By participating in our events and giving feedback

What is the response time for help requests?

We strive to respond to all requests within 24 business hours. For emergencies, we aim to respond within 2 hours during our operating hours (9 AM - 5 PM, Monday to Friday).

Can I suggest new topics or activities?

Absolutely! We love receiving suggestions from our users. Use our contact form or send us an email with your ideas. We'll review them and try to incorporate them into future updates.
